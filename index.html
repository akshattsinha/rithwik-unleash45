PERIODISATION

8-WEEK MACROCYCLE

A macrocycle is built step-by-step. Hover over each week to expand the exact biomechanical milestones, targets, and metabolic focuses.

Weeks 01 - 02

Neuromuscular Baseline

Establishing movement patterns, identifying unilateral deficiencies, and initiating structural connective tissue adaptations.

  • Deep hip mobility sequences
  • Core-bracing dynamics under static load
  • Eccentric muscle loading to stimulate tendon resilience

Hypertrophic Density
